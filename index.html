Introduction

AR storytelling is a new and immersive way to read stories.
This application provides you with the most advanced storytelling techniques using Augmented Reality.

AR Storytelling is a portfolio project using AR technology and storytelling techniques. It is aimed to help kids read with a higher degree of interest and engagement. Rather than reading the book silently, the children can play the animation with sound by scanning the bar code.
